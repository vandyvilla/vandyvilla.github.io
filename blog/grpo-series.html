Understanding GRPO

A three-part series on Group Relative Policy Optimization — the math, a from-scratch implementation, and a field reference for real training runs.

GRPOReinforcement LearningSeries ·June 2026 ·3 posts

GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization) is the reinforcement-learning algorithm behind DeepSeek-R1 and much of today's reasoning-model wave: it drops PPO's value critic and instead scores each sampled completion against the average of its group. These three posts cover it from complementary angles — theory, code, and operations. They're written to be read in order, but each stands on its own.
