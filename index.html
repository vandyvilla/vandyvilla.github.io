About Me

I'm a Senior Staff Software Engineer & Tech Lead Manager at Google Cloud AI, where I work on Gemini and Agent Quality. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Vanderbilt University and over a decade of industry experience, I specialize in building enterprise-grade AI systems at scale.

My work spans the full stack of LLM agent development -- from post-training models for function calling and tool use, to building evaluation benchmarks, agentic orchestration frameworks, and simulation/optimization systems. I've contributed to every major version of Gemini (1.0 through 3.0).

Before Google Cloud AI, I led engineering efforts at Roblox, Waymo, and Google Ads, architecting global-scale distributed services and ML data platforms.
